Events 
Each product at Truora has associated events that your system might find valuable for monitoring in order to trigger an action. Examples of such events include a Check completion, a Validation failure, WhatsApp conversation delivery, among others . In this guide, you will learn about each one of them. 

Variables 
Associated with each event is an object ; this object is related to the by-product and will contain varying levels of information depending on the trigger. For instance, the information about a validation at the beginning of the process will differ from the information at the end of the validation process. These objects provide the basis for the different available variables , which can be utilized to create conditions or for use in emails or HTTPS request actions. 
Disclaimer: Note that the availability of variables may vary for objects such as Checks , depending on the dataset configuration and the feasibility of variable calculation in the check’s country. 

Criminal and Legal variables 
Criminal and Legal variables require additional context in order to apply conditions for an event, along with special operators to express specific conditions. 
To utilize these variables , it’s essential to define their participation . They can be categorized as either Defendant , indicating being accused of , or Plaintiff , indicating accusing someone of . 
For these variables the has no records and has records operators are enabled. These operators allow you to verify the presence or absence of any legal or criminal background, respectively.
